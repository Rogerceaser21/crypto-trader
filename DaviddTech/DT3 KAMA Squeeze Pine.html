2 · What it does & how it trades

  • KAMA — a Kaufman Adaptive MA rolled by hand from the efficiency ratio (|ΔLclose| / Σ|Δclose|): smooth in chop, fast in trend. Entries need close above a rising KAMA.
  • Squeeze release — Bollinger Bands compress fully inside the Keltner Channel (a volatility squeeze); the entry trigger is the bar the squeeze lets go after being held ≥2 bars.
  • Momentum gate — a 16-bar LSMA of price vs its mid/mean composite must be positive and rising.
  • Efficiency gate — the efficiency ratio must exceed 0.20 (a directional, non-choppy move).
  • Fill — the open of the next 1h bar after the confirmed signal (causal). Long-only, flat-only (no pyramiding).
  • Initial stop — 2.8×ATR(14) below the signal-bar close, frozen for the life of the trade. Detected on the chart 1h low (identical exit bar to the engine’s 1m detection — a 1h low is the min of its 1m lows, and the stop is a fixed level).
  • Forced exit (dominant, ~70%) — when close < KAMA and momentum < 0, close at the next 1h open. The momentum flip is the real engine of the system.
  • Trail (rarely binds, ~1%) — a ratcheting close-based 5×ATR trail; exit when close ≤ trail. So wide the forced exit almost always fires first.
  • Sizing of record — 1%-of-equity risk against the initial stop distance (2.8×ATR at the signal bar). No leverage, no regime multiplier.

4 · Install on TradingView

  1. Open a BINANCE:BTCUSDT perpetual chart on the 1-hour timeframe — the exact feed and timeframe the edge was measured on.
  2. Load full history (scroll back to ~2019 or set a wide range) so KAMA and the squeeze/momentum indicators seed — the first ~300 1h bars are a warm-up and do not trade.
  3. Open Pine Editor, paste the indicator source (copy button below), Save, then Add to chart. It overlays the KAMA, marks entries/exits, shows squeeze dots and the status panel.

  5. To alert: right-click the chart → Add alert → Condition = this indicator → pick “LONG signal” or “exit” → Once per bar close. The entry alert fires on the signal bar; enter at the next 1h open.
  6. Leave the signal knobs on their validated center defaults. They are exposed as inputs so you can inspect — not so you re-tune the edge.
