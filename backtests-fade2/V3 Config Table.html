Q1 · Does BE@1R help or hurt at 2.5R live?

HURTS  −26.5R (−0.159R/trade, paired t = −2.48).

At the live default it drags +57.95R down to +31.42R. Win-rate collapses 45.5% → 31.7% as 41 would-be winners get scratched at break-even. Every BE-on cell is worse than its BE-off twin. Do not move to break-even.

Q2 · What does the SD gate cost / buy live?

COSTS  it throws away net winners.

Requiring the spike to cross an SD mean line drops 66 of 167 trades — and those dropped small-spike fades are the better book (+0.55R/trade, t = +2.60) vs the big-spike fades it keeps (+0.21R). The gate cuts +57.95R to +21.46R. Buys nothing; do not gate.

3 · How the SD gate was computed (causal, live lines — not a fixed 2.8%)

Igor's rule: a Monday spike qualifies only if its excursion from the weekend base crosses at least one of three Weekend-SD average lines. The gate is a faithful port of Weekend_Monday_SD_Levels_LIVE.pine:

  • Base = close at the dead-gap end (Monday 02:00 Dubai = Sunday 22:00 UTC) — the same base all the Pine's level lines share (close[1] at the boundary bar).
  • Three mean lines, per side: all-history UP 2.8076% / DOWN 2.7809% (Truth-Report constants); live 4-Monday mean; live 13-Monday (3-month) mean. The live means are computed causally from prior completed Mondays only — a Monday's own move never feeds its own lines; the rolling store keeps the last 13.
  • Cross test: crossing the smallest applicable line = crossing at least one. Pass if the spike's excursion% (from base to the running spike wick) ≥ that smallest line.

This is emphatically not a fixed 2.8% gate: the smallest line spans 0.30%–2.81% (median 2.11%), and in 110 of 167 weeks the binding line is a live sub-2.5% line; only 42 early weeks fall back to the history constant. Kept (pass) spikes average 5.16% excursion; dropped spikes 1.24%. The gate genuinely uses the live self-updating lines, exactly as the indicator draws them.

5 · Honest limits

  • In-sample only. 2019–2024-12-31. None of these cells has met the locked 2025-26 OOS. The recommendation is the IS-best live config, not an OOS-proven one — the single coordinated OOS event is still pending.
  • Streaming ≠ hindsight. These are the phantom-inclusive live entries; the number is honestly lower and noisier than the validated 91-trade hindsight ledger (+0.382R). This page measures what a real trader could actually have taken.
  • Spike-excursion definition. The gate measures the running spike wick (the stop wick at entry) as a % move from the dead-gap base. That is the causal quantity known at entry; a different excursion definition (e.g. the full Monday-window high) could shift a few borderline weeks, but the direction of the result — small spikes fade better — is large and unlikely to flip.
  • t-bars are raw. No multiple-comparison correction across the 8 cells / 12 paired tests. The BE-hurts and gate-costs findings are consistent in sign across every cell, which is stronger than any single t; but treat individual near-bar t-values (e.g. −2.48, +2.73) as descriptive.
  • One market. BTCUSDT perp only. The fade thesis is BTC-specific weekend structure.
